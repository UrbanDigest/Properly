Privacy Policy

Properly: Real Estate Price Estimator is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information or sensitive user data.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, log, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Financial, health, or authentication data
  • Location data or user browsing history
  • Website content or user activity

Use of Permissions

The extension requests access to the currently active tab to read the URL and auto-detect realestate.com.au listings for estimating property prices. No external communication or data transmission is made from this extension.

Data Sharing

No data is collected or shared with third parties. All operations are performed locally within your browser.

Changes to This Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, we will update the policy text here accordingly.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us via the support contact on the Chrome Web Store listing.
